What is Anjoui?

Cook

Have you ever wanted to share with the world your passion for food? Do you spend too much on groceries and want to split the cost? We at Anjoui provide a platform where you could sell your favorite dishes to recoup some of the cost of your groceries, while displaying your great cooking skills!

Diner

Why have take-out food when you can have fresh home cook meals at extremely affordable prices? Anjoui makes sure you will always get fresh home cooked meals, while meeting cooks in your community and trying out different dishes!
