What is Anjoui?

Cook

Have you ever wanted to share with the world your passion for food? Do you spend too much on groceries and want to split the cost? We at Anjoui provide a platform where you could sell your favorite dishes to recoup some of the cost of your groceries, while displaying your great cooking skills!

Diner

Why have take-out food when you can have fresh home cook meals at extremely affordable prices? Anjoui makes sure you will always get fresh home cooked meals, while meeting cooks in your community and trying out different dishes!

How does Anjoui work?

A local cook decides that he or she will be making a dish and posts it to Anjoui with information including ingredients, potential allergens, cost, number of portions, and pick-up or delivery time.

A diner logs into Anjoui on their mobile device and sees the cooks dish. The diner can reserve dish portions, pay, and communicate with the cook directly and securely from the application.

The food is either delivered to the diner or the diner picks up food from the cook. Once the transaction is complete, payment is sent securely to the cook through our system.

After the transaction, the diner has an opportunity to rate the cook and let everyone know how great the dish was.

Why Anjoui?

With locally purchased groceries and food cooked within the same day, Anjoui meals are fresh from head to toe. From the minute you place the order until you pick up or request a delivery, the food is in good hands.

Homesick and miss grandma’s homemade mac and cheese? Anjoui is here for you. Most of our cooks are ordinary people just like you and I, and their recipes will definitely make you feel like eating at home. And you get to taste their grandma’s secret recipe!

Our cooks are passionate about food and excited to share their creations with you. Making a profit is not their main drive.

About the Team

We are a diverse group of passionate students who all love to eat and bond over food. The idea for Anjoui was was born from Justin Saeheng, a Kellogg MBA candidate, who always cooked more than he needed for his usual dinner meal. From this idea, Anjoui has evolved and grown into a platform where cooks can offer their creations and diners can find their delicacies at an affordable price. Anjoui provides a win-win environment for both diners and cooks, where diners can enjoy delicious fresh home cook meals at an affordable price, while cooks do not suffer from having to throw out food.
